ABOUT US

Wondered where the name OSW comes from?  It all started 11years ago when both myself, John O 'Sullivan and Jerry Walsh, Plant Operators at the time came together combined our skills and experience to make up OSW.  We took the risk and decided to form a plant hire business, starting with the purchase of only 2 machines available for hire. We were determined to exceed.  We sacrificed and knew that with our combined determination, efforts and knowledge within the industry, we could achieve it all and we would be able to supply our customers future needs. We now have a large fleet of plant available for hire ranging from 0.75 Tonne Micro Excavators to 30 Tonne Excavators, Rubber Ducks, Dumpers and various plant attachments to suit all our Excavators.  Ranging from Hydraulic Breakers to Augers, Grabs and Munchers.  We also transport many of our own machines with our
8 Wheeler Beaver Tail Plant Lorries which comes with a 20Ton Meter Crane which are available on hire to assist our customers haulage needs aswell.

We are based in Edgware, Middlesex but our services are available to all areas of London and all surrounding Home Counties.  We serve clients both domestic and commercial companies such as Edmund Nuttall Ltd, Getjar Ltd, Atlantic Contracts Ltd, Mulroy Construction Ltd, Montway Ltd and many more Multinational Companies. We providing plant hire along with our skilled, experienced and qualified plant operators to various sites, including the Olympics 2012 in Stratford.    

We work closely with our customers to ensure everything runs smoothly from beginning to end of hire with our professional and reliable service.  Our customers always come back for our reliable service at competitive rates for that guaranteed excellent service.  We know going the extra mile, we would be able to provide that more personal service to ensure our customers satisfaction to meeting all their needs. 

We are CITB (Construction Industry Training Board) registered and Members of the CPA (Construction Plant Association).
